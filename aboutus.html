About Us

Ramona's Cakery began in 2005 in Nepean, Ottawa. We've always had a passion for baking and have been partnering with wedding coordinators for quite a while. Ramona's Cakery specializes in Designer Cake Decoration. and provide custom cakes for events such as weddings.
